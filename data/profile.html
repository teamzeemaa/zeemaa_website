Who We Are

We Started Zeemaa Because We Love Events.

Not just the business of it. We believe in building something that sustains itself, creates opportunities, and supports the families behind every team member and partner we work with.

The feeling of walking into a venue where everything runs smoothly, attendees move through without confusion, and the organizer can finally breathe. That moment is what drives us every day.

We are an event technology company headquartered in Saudi Arabia. From the moment your first attendee registers to the last person leaving the venue, we handle the complete technology layer of your event.

We do not hand you a software link and disappear. We show up, set up, and stay until it is done. When something goes wrong on the ground, it is on us to fix it fast and quietly, without disrupting your attendees or making you look bad.

Event technology as a dedicated managed service is still new in KSA. We are built for this market. We understand the scale, the language, the pace, and what Saudi events actually require on the ground.
